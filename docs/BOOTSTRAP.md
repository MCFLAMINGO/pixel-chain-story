## Chicken-and-egg — resolved

**Problem:** Kindling needs PIX; PIX needs illumination; illumination needs… someone to start.

**Start today (pick one):**

1. **Illuminate** — run a node, join sequencers, reveal pending light → earn **50 PIX**/pixel in era 0 (no dollars, no prior PIX).  
2. **Shine in** — lock $5 USDC / wire → PIX to your Personal Source from **bridge escrow** (escrow filled by people who already earned PIX; not new print at the moment of your buy).  
3. **Receive** — Kindling with someone who lit or shone in.  
4. **Build** — Light Credits (uncapped) so apps/SISO aren’t blocked by scarce PIX.  
5. **Register** — shine in mcflamingo.com / treasury without buying the money supply.

## Who fills the bridge escrow?

Illuminators and LPs who **earned** PIX and choose to provide liquidity.  
That is voluntary market structure — not a founder mint into a “treasury coin.”

## Pricing

- Pilot ingress quotes (`BOOTSTRAP_INGRESS_PIX_PER_USD`) are **operator quotes**, not a peg.  
- **Never** do `21_000_000 × quote` and call it valuation.  
- Early PIX can be nearly worthless in dollars while the picture still grows — same as early Bitcoin.
